About us

We are a group of student gamers, who decided to make a gaming site specifically for students. Using a cutting-edge formula((fun_factor*hours_played)/price) we can with a high precision decide if a game is worth a purchase based on the average students budget. Currently our team is only three people, but we hope to expand as soon as we start bringing in some of that bling. Our current team is Lars, Petter and Erik.
